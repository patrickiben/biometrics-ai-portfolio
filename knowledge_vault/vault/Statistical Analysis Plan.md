# Statistical Analysis Plan

The master statistical methods document for CP-101, governing analysis populations, endpoints, and the planned summaries for the SAD/MAD first-in-human design.

- **Governs:** population definitions, estimands and analysis conventions, safety/PK summary methods, handling rules, and the mapping from objectives to outputs. It describes methods and shells only — never results.
- **Owner:** lead biostatistician; reviewed by clin-pharm and medical, version-controlled and finalized before lock.
- **Connections:** derived from the [[Protocol]] objectives and indexed from the [[CP-101 Knowledge Hub]]. It drives the [[TLF Shells]] and the [[ADaM Specifications]] downstream, and defines the scope and triggers for any [[Interim Analysis (ops)]].

Any protocol amendment touching endpoints, populations, or dosing cascades into a SAP revision; downstream specs are not updated until the SAP change is approved.
