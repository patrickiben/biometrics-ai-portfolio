# Roles & Responsibilities

Defines who owns each deliverable, decision, and sign-off across the CP-101 biostatistics workstream.

- **Governs:** the RACI for SAP authorship, TLF specs, ADaM/SDTM programming, independent QC, and database-lock readiness. Names the responsible role (not the person) for each handoff so nothing falls between functions.
- **Owns:** the Lead Biostatistician maintains this note; the in-house programming team and the unblinded/blinded statistician roles are described here, including dual-programming and reviewer independence rules.
- **Connects to:** all role labels used elsewhere resolve back here. Programmers and reviewers should follow the file/variable standards in [[Naming Conventions]] when producing deliverables, and this note is reachable from the [[CP-101 Knowledge Hub]].

Keep this current whenever staffing or coverage assignments change; it is the source of truth for escalation paths during the SAD/MAD dose-escalation cycle.
