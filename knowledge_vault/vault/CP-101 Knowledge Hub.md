# CP-101 Knowledge Hub

Single entry point for the CP-101 biostatistics and study-operations knowledge base. Start here to navigate the study's design, analysis, data, and delivery artifacts.

- **Governs:** the index and cross-links for all CP-101 ops notes; it carries no requirements of its own, only pointers and current status.
- **Owner:** Lead Biostatistician (study ops lead), reviewed jointly with Data Management and Clinical Operations.
- **Design and analysis** flow from the [[Protocol]] into the [[Statistical Analysis Plan]], with data handled per the [[Data Management Plan]] and oversight per the [[Monitoring Plan]].
- **Conduct and integrity** are covered by [[Randomization & Blinding]] through [[Database Lock]], then reported via the [[CSR Plan]].
- **Coordination** lives in [[Milestones]], [[Roles & Responsibilities]], [[Naming Conventions]], [[Glossary]], [[Vendors]], and [[Lessons Learned]].
