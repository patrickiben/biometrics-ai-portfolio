# Drug Supply & Accountability

Tracks investigational product receipt, storage, dispensing, reconciliation, and return for CP-101.

- **What this governs:** Kit numbering and labeling conventions, chain-of-custody from depot to site, temperature/storage logging, dispensing logs tied to the blinded code list, and end-of-cohort reconciliation and destruction records. Accountability must remain blind-safe — logs reference kit IDs, never treatment.
- **Owner:** Site pharmacy maintains accountability logs; the independent unblinded pharmacist reconciles against the schedule from [[Randomization & Blinding]]. Supply quantities and resupply timing coordinated with [[Vendors]] (IP depot / labeling provider).
- **Connections:** Kit IDs map to the blinded code list defined in [[Randomization & Blinding]]; depot, labeling, and distribution responsibilities are itemized under [[Vendors]].

Status is Active — accountability logs update per cohort through the dosing period.
