# Lessons Learned

Running log of what worked, what slipped, and what to change for the next CP-101 cohort and future protocols.

- **Governs:** Capture of process findings across cohort cycling — transfer reliability, shell ambiguities, QC turnaround, and coordination friction across the distributed team. Each entry pairs an observation with a concrete corrective action and an owner.
- **Owner:** Study ops lead curates; any team member can file an entry. Reviewed at cohort boundaries and at study close-out.
- **Connects to:** Findings on data quality and signal handling feed updates to the [[Monitoring Plan]]; recurring file-format or timing issues drive fixes to [[Data Transfers]] specs and SLAs. Prioritized actions are tracked from the [[CP-101 Knowledge Hub]].

Keep entries blameless and specific — the goal is a tighter, more predictable cycle, not a post-mortem narrative.
