# Vendors

Lists the external service providers supporting CP-101 and the biostatistics-relevant interfaces with each.

- **Governs:** which vendor supplies which data stream, the contact/escalation owner per vendor, and the operational dependencies that feed our datasets. Scope here is the interface and timing, not contractual terms.
- **Owns:** the Study Ops Lead maintains the roster; the Lead Biostatistician confirms data-facing specifications.
- **Connects to:** incoming feeds are scheduled and reconciled per [[Data Transfers]]; safety-lab vendor specifics live in [[Central Lab]]; IP shipment and reconciliation sit with [[Drug Supply & Accountability]]. Reachable from the [[CP-101 Knowledge Hub]].

Update whenever a vendor, format, or named contact changes so transfer specs and reconciliation steps stay aligned with the right provider across the SAD/MAD cohorts.
