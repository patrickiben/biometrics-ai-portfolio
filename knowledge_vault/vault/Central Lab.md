# Central Lab

Reference note for the central safety laboratory vendor for CP-101. (PK bioanalysis — e.g. LC-MS/MS — is frequently run by a separate specialist bioanalytical lab; it is captured here only when the same vendor provides it.)

- **Scope:** Captures the lab's role, analyte panels, reference-range source, kit/logistics conventions, and the data-delivery contact for transfer issues. Reference only — operational transfer mechanics are not duplicated here.
- **Ownership:** Vendor relationship and SLA tracked by study ops; analytical content owned by the lab.
- **Connections:** Transfer formats, cadence, and reconciliation are handled in [[Lab Data Transfers]]. Contract terms, SLAs, and escalation contacts are maintained alongside other partners in [[Vendors]].

This note is the quick-reference landing page for "who is the lab and what do they deliver"; for how the data moves and gets reconciled, follow the linked transfers note.
