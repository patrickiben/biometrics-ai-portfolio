# Interim Analysis (ops)

Plans the operational mechanics of any interim safety look in CP-101.

- **Governs:** the ops scaffolding for an interim — data-cut conventions, output shells and the restricted package handed to the board, the unblinded-statistician firewall, and the timing relative to dose-escalation gates. This note covers process and shells only; no interim results or numbers are recorded here.
- **Owner:** independent unblinded statistician executes; study ops lead schedules against escalation milestones. **Status: Planned** — routine between-cohort dose-escalation safety reviews are continuous and handled by the unblinded firewall / SRC; this note concerns a *formal* interim analysis, activated only if one is pre-specified or a board requests it.
- **Connections:** triggered and reviewed through [[DSMB Operations]]; analysis methods and populations follow the [[Statistical Analysis Plan]]; the supporting data snapshot is coordinated with [[Database Lock]] conventions.

Where a formal interim is pre-specified, its timing, scope, and outputs are agreed before any data are examined; an unplanned, board-requested look follows the same firewall and documentation discipline.
