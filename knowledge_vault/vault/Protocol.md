# Protocol

Authoritative description of the CP-101 first-in-human SAD/MAD design in healthy participants; the source document all downstream specs must trace to.

- **Governs:** objectives, endpoints, dose-escalation rules, eligibility, and the assessment framework that every operational artifact inherits.
- **Owner:** Medical Lead and Clinical Operations, with Biostatistics contributing the design and analysis sections.
- The [[Statistical Analysis Plan]] operationalizes the protocol's endpoints into analysis methods and shells; the [[eCRF Specification]] and [[Schedule of Assessments]] translate visit and data requirements into collection.
- Oversight follows the [[Monitoring Plan]], and treatment-allocation integrity follows [[Randomization & Blinding]].
- Return to the [[CP-101 Knowledge Hub]] for the full artifact map. Any protocol amendment triggers a traceability review across these linked notes.
