# Monitoring Plan

Defines how CP-101 safety and conduct are monitored across the SAD and MAD dosing periods.

- **Governs:** the integrated safety monitoring approach for this first-in-human healthy-participant study — central/statistical review cadence, blinded reporting outputs, dose-escalation support packages, and the trigger conventions that route concerns to oversight bodies. Methods and shells only; no participant-level values leave the controlled environment.
- **Owner:** study ops lead with the clinical safety physician; biostatistics produces the monitoring outputs and review-ready listings.
- **Connections:** scope and visit structure follow the [[Protocol]]; the conventions for what constitutes an actionable signal live in [[Risk Signals]]. Process improvements feed back into [[Lessons Learned]]. Indexed from the [[CP-101 Knowledge Hub]].

Review frequency tightens around each escalation gate; outputs are version-controlled and timestamped so the audit trail is reconstructable.
