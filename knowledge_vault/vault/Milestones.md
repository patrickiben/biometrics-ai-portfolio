# Milestones

Single tracked timeline of CP-101 statistical and reporting milestones.

- **Governs:** Target dates and dependencies for the key ops gates — SAP finalization, shell sign-off, dry-run readiness, lock, unblinding, and reporting hand-off. Drives the planning cadence and flags slippage early so downstream work can re-sequence.
- **Owner:** Study ops lead maintains the master schedule with the lead biostatistician; the in-house programming team commits to delivery dates per gate.
- **Connects to:** [[Database Lock]] is the pivotal gate that releases final analysis; the [[Interim Analysis (ops)]] checkpoint between SAD and MAD escalation has its own readiness window; and the [[CSR Plan]] timeline keys off lock. Roll-up status is surfaced on the [[CP-101 Knowledge Hub]].

Dates are operational planning targets (e.g., lock targeted end of quarter), revised at each planning review; no result-dependent commitments.
