# DSMB Operations

Describes how the Data Safety Monitoring Board is convened and serviced for CP-101.

- **Governs:** DSMB charter logistics — meeting cadence (scheduled and ad hoc), the structure of blinded vs. unblinded review packages, the firewall between the unblinded statistician and the study team, and the documentation of recommendations (continue / hold / modify escalation).
- **Owner:** study ops lead coordinates logistics; an independent unblinded statistician prepares restricted outputs outside the study team's access.
- **Connections:** escalations packaged under [[Risk Signals]] feed the review agenda, and any formal stopping or escalation evaluation is run through [[Interim Analysis (ops)]]. Charter and contacts are indexed from the [[CP-101 Knowledge Hub]].

All board materials are tracked with access controls so the blind is preserved and decisions are fully auditable after the fact.
