TLF interpretation drafter skeleton

A Quarto + on-prem small-model skeleton that auto-drafts TLF narrative text. The model never sources a number — deterministic facts feed a number-validated draft, behind a human gate. The self-test rejects hallucinated numbers.

Seven named gates that make a clinical-biometrics deliverable delivery-confident — reproducibility, numeric-provenance (numbers from validated tools, never an LLM), CDISC conformance & integrity, SAS↔R double-programming parity, adversarial QC, regulatory/reporting standards, and AI-use governance.
