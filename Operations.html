Hours budget reconciliation — "find hours" R Shiny + multimodal

When an out-of-scope deliverable suddenly needs doing, where do the hours come from? A planned-vs-actual-vs-forecast (EAC) effort reconciliation: net findable = (Σ planned + contingency) − Σ EAC, then a decision tree — reallocate under-run slack → contingency → defer → change order. Delivered five ways from one shared R logic core: a visual guide (decision-tree + source-map diagrams, a live roll-up), a runnable R Shiny app, a shareable dashboard, a fillable Excel workbook, and a build-it walkthrough. Hours/effort only, no dollars; synthetic.
